SHEEMO BOT

A Discord Bot designed to help you keep yourself organized

ABOUT

Sheemo Bot is an advanced configurable Discord Bot providing a load of producitivity features for avid Discord users!

FEATURES

The COVID-19 pandemic has completely changed our lives. Everything, including our personal and work lives have been moved online. As a result, online applications such as Zoom, Slack, and especially Discord have risen in popularity and usage. With the Sheemo Bot, users can set specific times where the bot will send a daily update with the weather and news that corresponds to their location. Additionally, users can also set reminders for themselves, and will receive a reminder from the bot to get a drink of water every 30 minutes.
